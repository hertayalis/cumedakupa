Palia: Where To Get And Use Blueberry Bush Seeds

 Posted on June 4, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  657 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Don't beat around the bush when harvesting Blueberries that can be grown from Blueberry Bush Seeds, so here's how to get and use them in Palia.

First Published January 8, 2024, 10:37

Sweet with a slight acidity when eating them, Blueberries are a powerhouse ingredient that can be used in many ways in Palia. These delightful fruits can be harvested once players have planted Blueberry Bush Seeds, which players can get for free from doing quests and spending some money from stores.

Nevertheless, they need to level up their Gardening Skill to buy these seeds and cook up a storm to make some delicious desserts. For players who are seeking to get their hands on these delectable berried treasures, we’ve explained in this guide how players can get and best use the Blueberry Bush Seeds in Palia.

How To Get The Blueberry Bush Seeds In Palia?

Like Apple Tree Seeds, the Blueberry Bush Seeds are one of many obtainable seeds that can be used in conjunction with the Gardening Skill in Palia. These seeds are primarily for planting purposes, but acquiring them can be achieved in a handful of ways, some easier than others in-game.

What is considered the best way to get Blueberry Bush Seeds without having to spend any Gold is by completing Badruu's Friendship Level 3 quest, Music to My Ears. Players are to meet the quest's objectives as they help out Badruu, and after completing the quest, players can receive five bags of Blueberry Bush Seeds as rewards.

Another way that they can obtain Blueberry Bush Seeds, although a bit more challenging to do, is by exchanging Lucky Coins at Zeki's Wondrous Machine, which is found inside the General Store and the Underground market. Players are warned that their chances are extremely low, but possible to get seeds from the Wondrous Machine, as other items can be obtained from these Lucky Boxes.

Finally, players can spend Skill Medals earned from completing Gardening Weekly Challenges when they become unlocked after reaching Level 10 with the Gardening Skill. They can visit Badruu to access the Gardening Guild Store, where they can buy a bag of seeds for 45 Skill Medals each.

How To Use The Blueberry Bush Seeds In Palia?

Players can only use the Blueberry Bush Seeds by either planting them into the soil before adding some fertilizer and watering them for the Blueberry Bush to grow. They can also sell Blueberry Bush Seeds through the Shipping Bin and when visiting the cash register at Zeki's General Store, but we recommend planting them before they can harvest Blueberries.

Blueberries can, in turn, be used in multiple ways the first being to sell them also through the Shipping Bin and at Zeki's General Store for some Gold. Selling standard-quality Blueberries will net them 39 Gold each and 58 Gold for Star-Quality Blueberries.

They can also consume standard-quality Blueberries to receive 30 Focus and 45 Focus when eating Star-Quality Blueberries. Perhaps the best way to use Blueberries is to utilize them when cooking Blueberry Pie, the Celebration Cake, and Petit Fives, which all require this fruit as their quantities will vary depending on the recipe.

Players can also use Blueberries for crafting Blueberry Jam by placing one Blueberry into the Hopper of the Preserves Jar. However, players must wait patiently for the jam to complete crafting before it can be eaten, sold for some Gold, or gifted to Villagers who like and love receiving Blueberry Jam.

Finally, players can gift Blueberry Bush Seeds to Villagers who will appreciate receiving these seeds as gifts, including Badruu and Nai'o. Likewise, they can also give them to players who made Open Requests for this seed, where they can receive Renown for fulfilling players' requests.

Palia is currently available via the Epic Games Store and in Open Beta for PC, as players can install the game launcher detailed in this guide. The game is now available to download for Nintendo Switch as of 14th December 2023.
