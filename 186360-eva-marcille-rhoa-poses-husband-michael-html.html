Eva Marcille from RHOA Poses with Husband Michael Sterling and Their Three Kids in Adorable Family P

 Posted on July 9, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  444 words  |  Martina Birk

Eva Marcille and her family look absolutely adorable in a recently released photo. The snapshot features the newest member of the family, Maverick, and comes after they just moved into their new Georgia home.

Eva has had to face some controversy in the last couple of months, but the “Real Housewives of Atlanta” star is not letting anything stop her from enjoying much-needed family time.

Michael Sterling & Eva Marcille at the 9th Annual Celebration 4 A Cause Fashion Show on Dec. 22, 2016 in Atlanta, Georgia | Photo: Getty Images

In a new picture shared via Instagram, Eva and her husband, attorney Michael Sterling, were all smiles while holding their precious kids. The “America's Next Top Model” winning alum wrapped an arm around her daughter Marley Rae while Michael held the boys.

Eva also had to address another controversy, this time regarding her comments about some of the other housewives

Eva, 35, shares her five-year-old daughter with an allegedly abusive ex, Kevin McCall, while her Atlanta attorney spouse is the biological father of Michael Jr., 20 months, and Maverick, who will be three months old on December 27.

“Me and mine!!!!” Eva captioned the casual family photo that has garnered thousands of likes and comments from fans and celebrity pals alike.

“Beautiful Family,” gushed Eva’s friend and RHOA co-star Cynthia Bailey while another person chimed in: “Brilliant and Beautiful! What’s blessing! Happy Holidays Sis!”

The Sterling family recently had cause to celebrate after Eva finally found their new home. She took to Instagram late in November with a photo to announce that she “said yes to the address.”

RHOA fans will recall that Eva’s living condition was a hot topic last season after it was revealed that she and her family have been renting homes. The fashion model explained at the time that it was due to Kevin’s alleged stalking, but that did not stop the rumor mill.

Now that she’s bought a home in Alpharetta, Georgia according to Bravo’s Style & Living, Eva can’t wait to show off the beautiful interior of her family’s abode.

Per the outlet, Eva recently posted short video clips showing off her grey furniture color scheme, the open-concept floor plan of the house, and two Jerell Gantt paintings of Martin Luther King Jr., and Malcolm X.

Earlier this month, Eva also had to address another controversy, this time regarding her comments about some of the other housewives on the Bravo reality series.

Eva had called Kenya Moore and Porsha Williams “nappy heads” and also said Kenya had a “later in life baby.” She insisted, however, that none of those statements were meant to disrespect the women involved.
