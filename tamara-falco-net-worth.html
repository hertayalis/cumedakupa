What is Tamara Falcó's net worth? The Spanish socialite and fashion professional now has her own Netflix series.

It pays to be an accomplished socialite. Tamara Falcó, best known as the half-sister to Spanish singer Enrique Iglesias, has made quite the name for herself. And with such a lengthy resume, fans believe that her bank account has reaped the benefits. So, what is Tamara Falcó's net worth? Here’s what we know.

Tamara Falcó's net worth is steadily growing.
