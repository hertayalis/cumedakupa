Who is Ha Seok-jin?

Ha Seok-jin is a well-known South Korean actor with thousands of fans on social media. He is managed by Management Koo and has been nominated twice for the Excellence Award at the 2014 MCB Drama Awards.

Ha Seok-jin rose to prominence for his parts in dramas and films such as “Radiant Office,” “Crash Landing on You,” and “When I Was the Prettiest.”

He was most recently seen in the films Blind, The Dreamers, Suspicious Neighbor, and Local Dining Table. He’s also working on a project, Young Mob Protect’s Song.

Education, College

During his high school years, Ha Seok-jin was an above-average student. Apart from his academics, the only thing he was known for was his enthusiasm for acting which he demonstrated in the plays he appeared in.

He enrolled at Hanyang University’s Department of Mechanical Engineering and enlisted in the military after his first year.

Seok-jin began working as an engineer after graduation, but his exceptional looks and camera-friendly attitude led to him modeling for certain brands.

Ha Seok-jin: Career, Movies, Dramas

In 2005, a Korean Air campaign launched Ha Seok-jin’s career in the entertainment world. Although he had no intention of becoming an actor, he found it entertaining and got progressively drawn to it.

He signed with C-JeS Entertainment in 2019, however, his contract terminated in 2021, forcing him to look for new representation. He joined Management Koo in 2021.

His recent work, Blind

Blind is a 2022 Korean television series directed by Shyhin Ki-kyung and Jang Jeong-do that aired on tvN. Kwon Ki-kyung created this mystery, thriller, and crime series.

The narrative follows regular individuals who have become unjustly victims of various crimes, their perpetrators, and those who want to arrest the criminals. This series’ major cast members were Ok Taec-yeon and Jung Eun-ji.
