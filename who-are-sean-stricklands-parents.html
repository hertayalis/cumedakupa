Who are Sean Strickland’s Parents? – Sean Strickland is an American mixed martial artist currently holding the UFC Middleweight Championship.

He began his professional career in 2008 and previously held the King of the Cage Middleweight title. As of November 14, 2023, he ranks #8 in the UFC men’s pound-for-pound rankings.

Born on February 27, 1991, Strickland’s debut in the professional arena occurred in 2008 with King of the Cage, where he achieved an undefeated 9–0 record. His victory over Josh Bryant at KOTC: Unification in December 2012 secured him the King of the Cage Middleweight Championship. Subsequently, Strickland defended his title three times before joining the UFC.

Sean Strickland’s journey includes triumphs, setbacks, and a two-year hiatus following a motorcycle accident. In a significant upset, Strickland faced Israel Adesanya for the UFC Middleweight Championship on September 10, 2023, at UFC 293, winning via unanimous decision. This victory marked his third Performance of the Night bonus award.
