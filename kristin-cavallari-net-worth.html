Kristin Cavallari Wiki Biography

Kristin Elizabeth Cavallari, born on 5th January 1987 in Denver, Colorado USA, is a well-known American actress, television personality, and designer. She gained fame as a participant of a reality show and has been active in the entertainment industry since 2004.

Kristin Cavallari Net Worth $2.5 Million

Reportedly, Kristin Cavallari’s current net worth is $2.5 million, primarily accumulated from her acting and television appearances. Despite earning $90,000 per episode of “Hills”, she reportedly spent almost all her money on extravagant things like a house in Laguna Beach, USA, a Land Rover, and various luxury items.

Kristin’s childhood was tumultuous due to her parents’ divorce when she was young, leading her to experience multiple school changes. Despite this, she was invited to participate in her first television series, “Laguna Beach: The Real Orange County” (2004 – 2005), which catapulted her to fame. She continued her successful career by hosting another reality series, “Get This Party Started” (2006), and subsequently appeared in “The Hills” (2009 – 2010), “RuPaul’s Drag Race” (2011), “Dancing with the Stars” (2011), and other television shows.

Kristin has also made appearances in several films, such as “Fingerprints” (2006), “Green Flash” (2008), “Wild Cherry” (2009), and “National Lampoon’s Van Wilder: Freshman Year” (2009), which further contributed to her wealth and popularity.

Outside of her professional endeavors, Kristin actively engages in charitable activities to raise funds for vaccine development, HIV, and AIDS research. However, her family garnered negative attention for their decision not to vaccinate their children. Kristin has also undertaken charitable work in Kenya and El Salvador.

On a personal note, Kristin had tumultuous relationships with co-stars Stephen Colletti and Brody Jenner before embarking on a relationship with professional football player Jay Cutler, whom she married in 2013. The couple has two children.
