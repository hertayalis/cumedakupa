Nia Jax Buries Tamina For Allowing Others To Walk All Over Her

 Posted on July 11, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  294 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Nia Jax and Tamina Snuka seem to have a feud that goes deeper than family history. Those two have something to prove and neither is backing down.

Tamina tweeted out a video of her WrestleMania encounter with Nia Jax. She also included a caption to describe what fans were seeing in the clip.

“I’m King Kong bitch”, words uttered by @NiaJaxWWE to me at #Wrestlemania. U may be bigger & a bit stronger than me Sis, but I possess a resiliency you’ve never seen, the unmatched ferocity of a primordial beast, & last but not least, THE SAVAGERY OF A GOD…ZILLA. BITCH

Nia Jax wasn’t going to take that without sending a reply. The Irresistible Force fired a big shot at Tamina for allowing others to walk all over her for ten years. On the other hand, Nia Jax created her own spot in WWE.

What took so long for you to show this “unmatched ferocity”? For a decade, you allowed others to walk all over you, take advantage of your WEAKNESS! I came here, created my own opportunities & became a household name. If you think you can steal the legacy I created, you’re wrong

We’ll have to see how this situation turns out. Nia Jax and Shayna Baszler have been strong WWE Women’s Tag Team Champions, but Natalya and Tamina Snuka are also two wrestlers with a ton of determination to capture that gold.
